Personas

These product usage personas represent our customers using Cloud, Dedicated, Hybrid and Private Cloud infrastructures. The personas are built upon a collection of research done throughout the past year and a half. Personas will help you speak consistently about our customers, develop empathy, create real-world mappings of our customer base, recruit target users for products and prioritize design direction or solutions.

The personas will continue to evolve as we gather more quantitative and qualitative data about our customers. Read more about personas on design.rax.io.
